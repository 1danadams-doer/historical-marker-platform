## Initial Source Systems

### Texas Historical Commission CSV

Expected fields may include:

- MarkerNum
- Atlas_Number
- Title
- County
- City
- Location Description
- Marker Text
- Code
- Condition
- UTM Zone
- UTM Easting
- UTM Northing

### Texas Historical Markers GIS Shapefile

Expected fields may include:

- NAME
- LATITUDE
- LONGITUDE
- ATLAS_NUM
- MarkerNum
- last_edited
- Geometry
