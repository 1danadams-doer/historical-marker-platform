
# Historical Marker Platform

## Mission

The Historical Marker Platform exists to preserve, connect, and share the stories of people, places, events, organizations, and historical resources for future generations.

Historical markers are often the public entry point into local history. Behind every marker lies a larger story that includes photographs, documents, oral histories, buildings, landscapes, institutions, and community knowledge.

The goal of this project is to create a durable, open, and extensible platform that supports:

* Historical preservation
* Historical research
* Historical education
* Community stewardship
* Marker restoration awareness
* Open reuse of historical information

The platform is designed to support historical societies, museums, libraries, educators, researchers, volunteers, and the public.
